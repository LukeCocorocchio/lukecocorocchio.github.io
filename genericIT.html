Why I chose RMIT

I initially didn’t choose RMIT for any special reason, it was simply the fact that RMIT is considered as a moderately well-established university and the location in contrast of other universities was somewhat convenient. I’d also been somewhat familiarised of how RMIT works as the secondary school I had gone to provided students a day where we went to RMIT and where displayed some of their facilities. However, when I further researched into universities, I quickly realised that RMIT’s IT degrees are considered as one of the best that Melbourne has to offer, hence it was clear that I should strive to choose RMIT as one of my leading preferences for university selections.
